About This BMI Calculator

This free online BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator helps you quickly determine your body mass index based on height and weight. It supports both metric (cm/kg) and imperial (ft/lb) units.

This tool is designed for informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a doctor or health professional before making any health-related decisions.

We are an independent developer committed to providing simple, fast, and ad-supported tools to assist with everyday calculations.
