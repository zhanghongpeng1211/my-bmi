Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight.

2. What is a healthy BMI range?

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy for most adults.

3. How is BMI calculated?

BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²). You can use our calculator above.

4. Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a general guideline. It may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or the elderly.

5. Why should I use this BMI calculator?

It's free, fast, and supports both metric and imperial units.
